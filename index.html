Our Mission

To foster deep understanding of human civilization, critical thinking about contemporary political issues, and appreciation for cultural diversity among HISHK students. We create opportunities for students to engage with history and politics through debates, research, field trips, and meaningful discourse that prepares them for active citizenship.
