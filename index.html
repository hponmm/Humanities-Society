About the Humanities Society

A platform for students to extend their knowledge and expand their passion for the humanities

The Humanities Society is a space for discussion, debate, and reflection on the ideas and events that shape our world, with a particular focus on politics. While our sessions often centre on current affairs and political thought, we are also open to any suggestions on potential topics, either to be led by us, or by our members.

One of our main aims this year is to make the society not just group for discussion, but also a platform. If you have an interest you'd like to explore, whether that's writing an article, sharing a presentation, or hosting the competition, this is a place where you can do that. We want to encourage members to contribute their own perspectives, and to use the society as a way to develop and share ideas that go beyond the classroom.
