About the Humanities Society

A platform for students to extend their knowledge and expand their passion for the humanities

The Humanists Society is a student-led organization dedicated to reigniting passion for history, politics, philosophy, and cultural studies at Harrow International School Hong Kong. While interest in STEAM has continued to grow rapidly, interest in Humanities has begun to dwindle. We aim to change that by creating engaging opportunities for students to explore these subjects that form the basis of our culture and society.

Through weekly newsletters, competitions, debates, guest speakers, and field trips, we foster deep understanding of human civilization, critical thinking about contemporary political issues, and appreciation for cultural diversity. Our platform allows students to showcase their interests, engage in meaningful discourse, and prepare for active citizenship in our interconnected world.
